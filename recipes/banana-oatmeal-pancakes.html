Banana Oatmeal Pancakes

Delicious banana oatmeal pancakes made in a blender!

These banana oatmeal pancakes are super easy to make. You just need to add all of the ingredients to a blender, and let the blender do its magic! For this recipe, there is no need to include sugar and flour as the natural sugar in the bananas and the oatmeal is enough. Here is all you will need to make them:

Ingredients

  • 2 medium ripe bananas
  • 1 ½ cups old fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a blender and blend on high for about 30 seconds to 1 minute. Let the batter sit in the blender while you heat your pan up.
  2. Coat the pan slightly with olive oil and place over medium heat. Once the pan is hot, add ⅓ cup of the batter for each pancake. Cook each pancake for 2-4 minutes until they're puffed up.
  3. Flip the pancakes and cook until golden brown. Lower your heat if you find the pancakes are browning too quickly.
  4. Wipe the pan clean and repeat with more oil and remaining batter.
